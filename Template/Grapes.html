Fruit Of The Day- Grapes

  • Grapes are a wonderful source of micro-nutrients like copper, iron, and manganese, all of which are important in the formation and strengthening of bones.
  • The high levels of Vitamin C, K, and A in grapes gives your immune system a healthy boost.
  • Flavonoids present in grapes are antioxidants, which can reduce and fight the damage caused by free radicals.
